Orange-Blueberry-Yogurt Parfaits

This Yogurt Parfait is sweet and a little tart, with a nutty hint from the granola. TEXTURE: The mix of creamy yogurt, juicy berries, and crunchy granola is like a party in your mouth.

Ingredient

  • ¾ cup vanilla yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon grated orange zest
  • 2 large fresh strawberries, sliced
  • ¼ cup maple nut granola
  • ½ cup fresh blueberries

Directions

  1. Mix yogurt and orange zest together in a bowl.
  2. Layer some of the strawberries, yogurt mixture, granola, and blueberries in a parfait glass. Repeat layers until all the ingredients are used up.
